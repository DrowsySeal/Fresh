FAQs

What premades do you have available?

Available premades will be posted here, and announced on our Telegram channel and socials.

How do I commission you?

Commission openings will be announced on our Telegram channel, as well as on all our socials.

To apply for a commission slot, you must have a fursuit friendly reference sheet and submit an offer. Please note that commission slots are not first come, first served.

For futher details, please see our Commissions page.

How old do I have to be to purchase from you?

You must be 18 years of age or older. We do not work with minors, even with parental permission.

Do you accept payment plans?

It depends. In some situations, we can accept a 50% down payment plan, to be paid off within a shorter timeframe.

Do you take commissions for just eyes?

No, sorry!

Where do you get your materials?

We get our furs from Howl Fabrics, and mochi minky is usually sourced from Hopen Textiles. Our eye mesh is computer mesh custom ordered from Alibaba.

What is your turn-around time?

Premades and in-stock items will ship within 3-5 business days of payment.

Commission times depend on the project, and are also affected by how many orders are in our queue. We do not keep a long queue, but we do work in order of payment received.

If you have a certain deadline that needs to be met, please let us know before commissioning. We will do our best to work with you!
