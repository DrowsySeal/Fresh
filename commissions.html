Commissions

Applications

When commissions are open, you may submit your application via the designated form. I accept quotes, which are available at any time here, or budget-based offers, starting at or above my base pricing.

The form will ask you to submit a fursuit-friendly reference sheet, the type of fursuit you are looking to get, and your PDF quote and/or offer.

I select projects on a case-by-case basis, with how well the design will work with my style. If I am interested in your project, I will reach out to discuss details and expectations, and schedule you for a slot.

You must be 18 years of age or older to apply for a commission.

    Reference sheet requirements:
  • Fursuit friendly, with simplified spots and stripes, and no gradients
  • Safe for work. NSFW reference sheets will be ignored
  • Clean and unshaded, human-created artwork
  • Two or more full anthro body views, showing the front and back at minimum
  • Tongue, paw pads, claws, and other details must be included
  • The character must be yours. I will not replicate copyrighted characters
  • Character changes are not accepted after commission acceptance, so please make sure your reference sheet is accurate!

Acceptance
  • Once your commission is accepted, a 50% down payment is required within 48 hours. The remaining 50% must be paid off within 30 days.
  • Once your down payment has been paid, you will be officially added to my queue! c:
Turnaround
  • My queue is kept very short, and will be worked in order of full payment received.
  • I aim to keep our queue under a 6 month turnaround time from full payment date to completion.
  • I will do my best to work with a specific timeline, but please understand that con deadlines are not accepted.
  • In order to keep turnaround times short, I keep the official queue down to 2-3 slots.
Progress

I pride myself in excellent communication, and will include WIP photos at every major step of the commission progress. Please be prompt in approval or with any desired changes, as a delay in responding may result in an inaccurate completion time.

    Steps include:
  • Digital reference creation
  • Picking out furs and fabrics
  • Headbase creation
  • Bodysuit 3d modeling (if applicable)
  • Taping and patterning
  • Eye design
  • Details
  • Final photos

Please note: For fullsuit commissions, I do not work off DTDs! I require around 20 measurements and three photos of you from the front, side, and back. These must be taken in form-fitting clothing to ensure proper fit. I will send documents immediately before beginning your project, so please fill these out and return them in a timely manner.

Shipping
  • Shipping costs must be covered by you at the completion of the project.
  • Shipping within the US can range from $60 to $200 or more, depending on the size of the project.
  • Please view our Shipping Terms for more information.
